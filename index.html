How does it work?

Value Seeker

A person looking for an opportunity to contact people they value, but do not know how to identify these people and approach
Examples
You are working on a new product and would like to get a critical feedback from a potential customer who you value but have no direct contact or reference.

You would like to meet an extremely busy CEO, CTO of a company to explain a potential business partnership that would be mutually benefitial

You wish to get a well respected person as your mentor for a project and would like to talk to that person

Value Provider

A well accomplishd person who is highly respected for their knowledge and skills which are in high demand. They are leaders in their domain and can be a change catalyst.
Examples
You are an important person - CEO, VP or anyone holding a critial position in a company, or a powerful political player, or simply a celebrity. Most often the truth is people will be ready to pay just to talk to you.

You are one of the top influencers in social media (with huge following and connection in Twitter, LinkedIn, blog, etc) . You are a true leader with potential to make huge impact.

OpenDoor. Creating Win-Win Outcome

As a Value Provider you can monetize your value. You set a minimum reward amount for contacting you directly and provide opportunity for people, who values you and your time, to connect with you directly.

As a Value Seeker you can find your Value Provider and directly contact them by paying their minimum reward amount. You take complete controle of your opportunity for success.




Summary

OpenDoor is a platform that creates opportunity for people to easily connect with people they value. And help people with strong social influence to benefit from what they have achieved. The platform unlocks a lot of possibilities and opportunities creating win-win outcomes.
